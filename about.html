Who I Am

I am a Computing Educator and DevOps Engineer based in the United Kingdom. With a strong foundation in education and a passion for technology, I bring a unique perspective that bridges the gap between teaching and industry practice.

My career spans roles in ICT management, network administration and computing education. I hold multiple qualifications including a Master of Technology degree and Qualified Teacher Learning and Skills (QTLS) status.
